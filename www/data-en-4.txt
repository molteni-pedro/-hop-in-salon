<p>
    Concentrate for colour-treated hair, formulated with natural antioxidants, shine enhancers and colour sealing polymers to improve brightness, make hair colour last longer and protect it from oxidative stress.

</p>
<h3>Benefits:</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Makes hair colour last longer</li>
    <li>Protects against radiation</li>
    <li>Enhances the vibrancy and shine of hair</li>
</ul>

<h3>Key ingredients:</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Natural antioxidants from green tea that protect hair from loss of colour due to oxidation from both the formation of free radicals and the effect of UV radiation.</li>
    <li>Quaternised polymer that evenly coats the hair shaft, creating a protective film that protects it against loss of colour from washing.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Directions for use:</h3>
<p>
    Based on a prior diagnosis, the professional combines the two steps – Intensifier & Recharge – in the applicator bottle. The blend is applied to hair after washing, leaving it to work for 7 minutes. 

</p>
